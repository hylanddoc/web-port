Driving School Project

A site to manage my driving instruction business as an alternative to commercially available applications.

The site was designed for use on a netbook where page scrolling was to be minimised.

The home page would contain all the necessary information rquired for the days instruction and liinks to assoiated sections such as adding a new booking, viewing and updating puipils progress record, adding payment records etc. As well as any messages/reminders that could potentially affect lessons or travel between lessons.
