Yorkshire & Humberside Boys' Brigade Project

A static site to advetise the Yorkshire & Humberside District of The Boys' Brigade.

As I was maintaining the Website for The Boys' Brigade in Hull and East Yorkshire, I was asked by the distrct to design and maintain a new site for them.

The site was a intended as a static site, updated reqularly with items of news. One of it's main functions was to provide details of The Boys' Brigade groups within Yorkshire and North Lincolnshire. In addition to pages providing information such as addresses and contact details, it also made use of a Google Map API to display a map showing the location of all the groups in the area.
